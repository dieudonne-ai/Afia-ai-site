About the Project

AFIA-AI (AI For Integrated Healthcare) is an innovative healthcare management system that uses artificial intelligence to improve the quality of medical care and optimize patient management.

The project integrates cutting-edge technologies such as predictive analytics, automated medical data processing, and intelligent recommendations based on patient history.

Our goal is to create a comprehensive solution that helps healthcare professionals make better decisions, save time, and provide personalized care to each patient.

Core Features

AI Analysis

Intelligent analysis of medical data with personalized recommendations using Claude API

Patient Management

Complete patient record management system with detailed medical history

Appointments

Appointment scheduling and tracking with automatic email notifications

PDF Export

Automatic generation of professional medical reports in PDF format

Dashboards

Advanced visualizations with interactive charts for statistics tracking

Document Management

Upload and secure storage of medical documents per patient

Full traceability of all actions with precise timestamps

Security

Secure authentication and protection of sensitive data

About the Research Method

Research method : The project is developed using a combination of research and development methodologies. Initially, a comprehensive literature review was conducted to understand the current state of healthcare management systems and the potential applications of artificial intelligence in this field in Africa. Based on this research, we designed a system architecture that integrates various AI technologies to meet the specific needs of healthcare professionals.
